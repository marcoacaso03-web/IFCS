# IFCS 2026 Data Challenge 

Data Dictionary — Italian SMEs, FY2023 

One row per firm. All monetary figures are in thousand euros (k€). Column names below match the CSV files exactly. Variable Description Unit / Format Notes 

Company ID Unique firm identifier. Number 

Province Italian province where the company is legally registered. Text Geographic reference (Italy). 

Sales Revenue Total income generated from the company’s core business activities through the sale of goods and services. k€ Nominal value, not adjusted for inflation. 

Employees Total number of company employees. Number 

Net income Final profit after all operating costs, financial expenses, and taxes have been deducted. k€ Can be negative. 

Operating Income Profit generated from core business operations, excluding financial costs and taxes. k€ Can be negative. 

Maximum deductible amount 

Maximum tax-deductible amount. k€ 

Total financial expenses 

Costs incurred for external financing, including interest payments and other debt-related charges. k€ 

Tax shield Tax benefit arising from the deductibility of financial expenses. k€ 

Operating cash flow 

Cash generated by the company’s core business activities. k€ Can be negative. 

Current taxes Income taxes accrued in the current period. k€ 

Alert Index Composite early-warning risk indicator signalling proximity to a critical risk threshold. Index Mixed type 

Ateco ATECO (ATtività ECOnomiche) code: the classification adopted by ISTAT to categorise economic activities. Number 

sector Sector of activity of the company, based on the ATECO classification. Text 

Financial distress Binary target: TRUE if the firm is in financial distress, FALSE otherwise. Logical Response variable, this is what you predict for the test.
